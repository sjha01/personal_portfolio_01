About Me

I am a Seattle-based data scientist, web developer, and physicist, with interests in naturalism and artistic expression. Some of my past work and current interests include:

  • Scientific research: I have studied Quantum optics, diffractive optics, quantum dots, and ionic liquids.
  • Machine learning: I have developed machine learning packages and statistics libraries for work done in the semiconductor industry.
  • Web development: I am developing apps for nature exploration and idea development and prototyping.
  • Nature exploration: I am birder, mushroom hunter, rockhound, and amateur botanist and geologist.
  • Art: I enjoy learning new crafts, and applying them to create functional artwork. Some interests that I work into these projects include woodworking, drawing, and painting.
